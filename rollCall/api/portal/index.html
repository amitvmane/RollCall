📋 My Groups — help

This portal shows your attendance across every RollCall group you've voted in — no separate login, just your verified Telegram identity.

Groups list

Each card is one group. The badge shows whether a rollcall is 🟢 open right now. Tap a card to open its detail drawer.

Attendance & streaks

Your attendance % is the share of finished rollcalls you marked in. The 🔥 flame is your current streak of consecutive attendances; your personal best is the longest streak you've ever had. Cancelled rollcalls don't count against you.

Upcoming

Scheduled rollcalls that haven't started yet appear at the top so you can plan ahead.

Identity

Use Unlink to detach your Telegram identity from this browser. You can re-verify any time with the Verify with Telegram button.

Need the bot commands? Run /help in any group.
